Welcome to a comprehensive learning guide for Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML). This repository provides structured pathways, resources, and career guidance to help you navigate the exciting world of AI and ML.

## Getting Started

Whether you're a beginner looking to start your journey in AI/ML or an experienced professional wanting to deepen your expertise, this guide offers structured learning paths tailored to different experience levels.

Choose your starting point based on your current skill level:
- **Beginner**: Start with [AI/ML Fundamentals](./ai_ml_fundamentals.md)
- **Intermediate**: Jump to specific roadmaps ([AI](./ai_roadmap.md) or [ML](./ml_roadmap.md))
- **Advanced**: Explore [Industry Applications](./industry_applications.md) or [Interview Prep](./interview_prep.md)
